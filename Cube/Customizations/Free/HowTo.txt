Description:
Here you will find some useful examples for how to write MDX-queries in Teleopti PM.

Example/howto:
"how turn a messure [Scheduled Time m] into format [hh:mm:ss]"
1) Drag the messure [Scheduled Time m] into a PM report
2) Click the little arrow next to the messure => "Add Formula" => "Add MDX Expression ..."
3) Paste the content of "Format-hhmmss (Scheduled Time m).mdx" into the dialog
4) Give a name to your custom messure e.g: hh:mm:ss
5) OK
